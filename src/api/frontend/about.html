Why Thrift Fashion Matters

🌱 The Problem with Fast Fashion

The fashion industry is the second-largest polluter globally, contributing to:

  • 10% of global carbon emissions
  • 20% of global wastewater
  • 85% of textiles ending in landfills
  • Microplastic pollution in oceans

💚 The Thrift Solution

By choosing second-hand fashion, you're:

  • Extending garment lifespan
  • Reducing demand for new production
  • Saving natural resources
  • Supporting circular economy

Carbon Footprint: Fashion Edition

📊 Average Carbon Emissions

New Jeans: 33.4 kg CO₂

New T-Shirt: 7.0 kg CO₂

New Dress: 17.2 kg CO₂

New Jacket: 35.8 kg CO₂

🌿 Thrift Impact

When you buy second-hand:

  • 82% less carbon emissions
  • 87% less water usage
  • 92% less textile waste

💡 Fun Fact: If everyone bought one used item instead of new this year, we'd save 5.7 billion pounds of CO₂ - equivalent to taking 76 million cars off the road!
