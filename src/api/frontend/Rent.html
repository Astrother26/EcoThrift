EcoThrift Rental Terms & Conditions

1. Item Care & Responsibility: The Renter agrees to take full responsibility for the rented item during the rental period. Items must be handled with care and returned in the same condition as received, excluding normal wear.


2. Return Policy: Items must be returned by 6:00 PM on the agreed end date. Late returns will incur an additional charge of ₹200 per day beyond the agreed return date, automatically deducted from the security deposit.


3. Damage & Loss Policy: Any damage beyond normal wear or loss of the item will be charged to the Renter at the item's current replacement value. EcoThrift reserves the right to assess damage independently within 48 hours of return.


4. Cleaning Requirements: Items must be returned clean. If EcoThrift determines that professional cleaning is required due to the Renter's use, cleaning costs will be deducted from the security deposit. Do not attempt to wash garments at home without checking the care label.


5. Security Deposit: A refundable security deposit equal to 30% of the item's retail price is collected at the time of rental confirmation. This deposit will be refunded within 7 business days after successful item return and inspection.


6. Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made 48+ hours before the rental start date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours of the rental start will forfeit 50% of the rental fee. No-shows forfeit the full rental fee.


7. Alterations Prohibited: Permanent alterations, repairs, dry-cleaning, or washing of rented items is strictly prohibited unless expressly approved in writing by EcoThrift.


8. Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in the Renter's city.
